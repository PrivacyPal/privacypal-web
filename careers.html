The process

Fast, fair, real.

We aim to make a decision inside 14 days from your first conversation. No homework that takes a weekend. No panels of seven. No ghosting.

  1. 01

    Apply

    Submit the form on the role page. We read every application — yes, even the founders' inbox.

  2. 02

    Intro call

    30 minutes with the hiring manager. Why this. Why now. What you'd want to own.

  3. 03

    Working session

    One or two sessions on real work. We share the prompt 48 hours ahead. We pay for your time on extended exercises.

  4. 04

    Team conversations

    You meet two-to-four future teammates. We learn how you collaborate. You learn what you'd actually be saying yes to.

  5. 05

    Founder & offer

    Final conversation with a founder. References. Offer letter. Real numbers, no negotiation games.
