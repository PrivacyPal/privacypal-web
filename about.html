About PrivacyPal

We believe you shouldn't have to choose between AI and privacy.

PrivacyPal was founded by a team of privacy engineers, cryptographers and former CXOs who kept running into the same wall: every AI tool worth using asks for your data, and every company worth protecting can't give it.

Our story

Built from both sides of the desk.

Our co-founders spent a decade between them shipping AI systems at scale — and a decade before that pioneering regulated data and financial systems that now form the standards in their industries. They watched teams build shadow-IT workarounds because the "right" answer was always "no." They built PrivacyPal so the answer could finally be "yes, safely."

Today we protect tens of millions of sensitive elements across finance, healthcare, law, telecom and technology — from family kitchens to trading floors to NOC war rooms.

Values

Three principles, one bar.

Privacy is the product

Not a checkbox. Not a page on the website. The thing we ship. Every roadmap decision answers to it first.

Default-on, not opt-in

The safe path has to be the easy path. If a user has to think about privacy, we've already lost.

Open to inspection

Third-party audits. Published detection benchmarks. Public bug bounty. Trust is earned, not asserted.
