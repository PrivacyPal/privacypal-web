About PrivacyPal · the mission

The governance & enablement layer for the Age of AI.

PrivacyPal exists so organizations can embrace AI without compromising their most sensitive assets. We're building the first AI-native security agent protecting a company's interests across the emerging AI Operating System — on-device, agent-aware, language-based by design. The CISO stops being the blocker. The business moves at AI speed.

Our story

Built from both sides of the desk.

Our co-founders spent a decade shipping AI systems at scale — and a decade before that pioneering regulated data and financial systems that now form the standards in their industries. They watched CISOs become the blocker by default, teams build shadow-AI workarounds to ship, and "secure" tools degrade UX so badly the team routed around them. They built PrivacyPal so security teams could finally say yes.

Today we govern tens of millions of sensitive elements across financial services, healthcare, legal, telecom and B2B technology — from a 40-seat firm to enterprises at agentic scale. The long-term vision: PrivacyPal as the first AI-native security agent for the AI Operating System.

Principles · 3 we live by

Three principles. One bar.

01 · Enable, don't block

The CISO becomes an enabler. AI velocity goes up. Sensitive data stays sovereign. If our product slows the team down, we've shipped the wrong thing.

02 · AI-native, not bolted on

Built from the ground up for how AI actually works — language, prompts, agents, MCP, edge models. Not legacy DLP wrapped in a new logo.

03 · Zero-knowledge by default

We never see your unencrypted data. Privacy Twins preserve statistical bandwidth without surfacing the original. Trust is earned in architecture, not in a marketing page.
