How It Works

Protection at the Edge

Real-time redaction, synthetic replacement, seamless re-injection. Your data never leaves your control.

Dynamic Data Redaction

PII, PCI, PHI, and proprietary IP are identified and masked in real time—before data leaves your corporate perimeter. The AI never sees it.

Privacy Twins

Our core breakthrough. We replace sensitive data with statistically identical synthetic Twins that mirror structure and relationships. The LLM maintains 100% contextual awareness and reasoning accuracy without ever touching the real information.

Advanced NLP & Heuristics

Lightweight, localized ML models understand context—not fragile regex. We identify complex IP and nuanced PII that pattern-matching tools miss.

Contextual Re-injection

When the AI responds, PrivacyPal seamlessly unmasks data for you. Flawless experience. Zero friction. You see your real data; the AI never did.

Agnostic Integration

Model-agnostic. One unified security layer across all LLMs. Use ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Copilot—no vendor lock-in.

Immutable Audit Ledgers

Every action logged in a tamper-proof ledger. Instant compliance reporting for SOC2, HIPAA, GDPR.
