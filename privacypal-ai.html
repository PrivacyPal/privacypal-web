How It Works

Control Before the Prompt Leaves Your Device

Unlike reactive security tools that alert after the fact, PrivacyPal governs AI usage at the point of intent — before anything reaches an AI server.

1

Intercept

PrivacyPal's lightweight on-device agent monitors every AI interaction in real time — across browsers and desktop apps — checking whether the tool is approved and who is using it.

2

Govern & Redirect

If the tool is approved, the prompt proceeds. If not, the user is instantly redirected to a sanctioned alternative. Sensitive data is classified and replaced with synthetic stand-ins.

3

Log & Report

Every AI interaction — approved or blocked — is logged to your governance dashboard. See who used what, when, and what data was involved. Full auditability, zero surprises.
