The Product Suite

The "How"

1. AI Gateway

The Smart Firewall for Prompts

A secure proxy layer between your employees and LLMs (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini). Automatically replaces PII and sensitive IP from prompts in real-time. Ensures your data never trains public models while maintaining a seamless user experience.

The "Pal" Edge: 0.10ms latency. Your users won't even know it's there; your auditors will be glad it is.

2. DSPM

AI-First Data Discovery

Continuous, agentless scanning of your cloud and on-prem databases to find "toxic" data combinations. Most tools tell you where data is; we tell you if that data is being sucked into a RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) pipeline by mistake.

The "Pal" Edge: It's not just a map; it's a mission control for your AI data lineage.

3. AI Security Platform

The Central Governance Hub

Your "Single Pane of Glass" for AI risk, usage analytics, and policy enforcement. Instantly generate board-ready reports for EU AI Act, ISO 42001, and NIST RMF compliance. Monitor token spend and identify which departments are using unauthorized "Shadow AI."

The "Pal" Edge: Moves you from "snapshot" compliance to continuous runtime governance.

4. Developer SDK

Privacy-as-Code

A lightweight library (Python, Node.js) for devs to build privacy-first AI apps from day one. Stops the "Security vs. DevOps" war. Developers get "Privacy-Safe" data hooks (like Privacy Twins) so they can build high-utility apps without ever touching raw PII.

The "Pal" Edge: Built-in semantic guardrails that score output risk before it hits the end user.
