The Process

Deploy Once. Control Everything.

Device Control runs silently on every team device — browser and desktop — enforcing your AI policies and reporting back to a single governance dashboard in real time.

1

Install in Under 5 Minutes

Push the PrivacyPal agent to your team via MDM or a download link. No VPN. No network reconfiguration. No IT ticket required. One installer covers browser extension and desktop app.

2

Every AI Interaction is Logged

From the moment the agent is active, every AI prompt — in every browser, every desktop app, every SaaS tool — is logged with the app used, data types detected, and any policy actions taken.

3

Admin Sets the Rules

Define which AI tools are permitted, which data types are blocked, and which users or teams have elevated access. Rules apply instantly across every enrolled device the moment they're saved.

No gaps. No shadow AI usage. Full visibility regardless of how your team accesses AI.

Policy Engine

Block the Apps You Don't Allow. Automatically.

Define an approved list of AI tools for each team or role. When an employee tries to use an unauthorized AI app — even a new one — Device Control blocks the connection at the network level and logs the attempt.

No employee re-training. No policy documents that nobody reads. The rules are enforced at the device level, every time.

  • Per-team and per-role AI tool allow lists
  • Block or warn mode for unauthorized AI apps
  • Automatic classification of new AI tools as they emerge
  • Integrates with your existing IdP and MDM
Audit & Compliance

Audit-Ready Logs. Always On.

Every AI interaction is timestamped, attributed to a user, and tagged with the data types detected. When a compliance review or security audit comes up, your logs are already organized and ready to export.

PrivacyPal Device Control makes it easy to demonstrate exactly what your team has and hasn't sent to AI tools — with the granularity your auditors actually need.

  • Immutable log storage with configurable retention
  • SOC 2 and HIPAA-aligned audit trail format
  • CSV and JSON export for third-party SIEM integration
  • Data residency controls for EU and other regulated regions

Pre-Audit AI Inventory

Auditors are increasingly asking organizations to account for AI tool usage. Device Control gives you a complete, exportable record before the audit request arrives.

HR & Legal

Sensitive Role Policy Enforcement

Some roles — HR, Legal, Finance — handle data that should never enter a third-party AI system. Device Control lets you define stricter policies for specific teams without affecting everyone else.

HR team is restricted from uploading documents to consumer AI tools. Attempts are blocked and logged — no employee conversation required.
Engineering

Developer Toolchain Governance

Developers use more AI tools than any other team — Copilot, Cursor, Codeium, Claude, and more. Device Control gives engineering managers visibility without micromanaging individual workflows.

An engineering org approves GitHub Copilot and Cursor but blocks direct Claude API calls without code review. Policy is enforced automatically across 40 engineers.
Sales & Marketing

CRM and Client Data Protection

Sales reps regularly paste CRM data, client lists, and pipeline information into AI tools. Device Control ensures this data is handled by PrivacyPal's Privacy Twins layer before it reaches any AI.

A sales manager pastes a client list into ChatGPT. Privacy Twins automatically replace all real client data before the request is sent — Device Control confirms the policy was applied.
Remote & Distributed Teams

Consistent Policy Across Locations

Remote teams across multiple regions often use different tools and operate under different compliance requirements. Device Control lets you maintain one policy baseline with per-region overrides.

EU-based employees automatically operate under a stricter GDPR-aligned policy. US and EU data residency rules are enforced at the device level — no manual configuration per employee.
